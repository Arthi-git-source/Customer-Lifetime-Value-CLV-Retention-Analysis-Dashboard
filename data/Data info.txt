Dataset Information – Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) & Retention Analysis

This dataset contains transactional and behavioral data of customers from an e-commerce platform over a 12-month period.

Total Records: ~10,000+ transactions
Total Customers: ~1,200 unique users

Columns Description:

* Customer_ID: Unique identifier for each customer
* Transaction_ID: Unique ID for each purchase
* Transaction_Date: Date of purchase
* Purchase_Amount: Total amount spent per transaction
* Product_Category: Category of purchased product
* Payment_Method: Mode of payment (UPI, Card, COD)
* Region: Customer location/region
* Tenure: Duration of customer relationship (in months)

Derived Fields (Created during analysis):

* CLV (Customer Lifetime Value): Total revenue generated per customer
* Retention Rate: Percentage of returning customers
* Churn Flag: Indicates inactive customers based on last purchase date
* Customer Segment: High, Medium, Low value classification

Purpose:
The dataset is used to analyze customer purchasing behavior, estimate lifetime value, identify churn patterns, and support data-driven retention strategies.
